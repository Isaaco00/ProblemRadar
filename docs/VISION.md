# ProblemRadar Vision

## Vision

Become the world's most trusted platform for discovering real human problems.

## Mission

Help people stop guessing by transforming millions of online conversations into evidence-backed opportunities.

## Motto

Don't build what people say they want.
Build what the evidence proves they need.

## Principles

- Evidence over assumptions.
- Solve real problems.
- Global by design.
- Trust through transparency.
- Simplicity first.
- Ship fast.
- Learn faster.

## Version 0.1 Goal

Help a user discover the top recurring problems for any topic from real online discussions.
